About Me

Hello! I'm a software developer based out of Calgary, AB passionate about building elegant solutions through code and creativity. With over two years of hands-on experience in full-stack development, I bring a well-rounded mix of technical and soft skills alongside a keen eye for details to every project I undertake.

Having graduated from the University of Calgary with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, I have had the opportunity to work on diverse projects in my career so far ranging from e-commerce platforms to data-driven dashboards and web applications. I prioritize clean and efficient code practices, test-driven development, and agile methodologies to deliver high-quality software solutions. I specialize in building web applications, APIs, and user interfaces using technologies such as Bootstrap, JavaScript (React, Angular), Java (Spring Framework), Python, WordPress and databases like MySQL and MongoDB.

I'm a lifelong learner who is constantly improving my skillset and learning best practices to stay at the forefront of the software landscape. Beyond technical skills, I value effective communication, teamwork and a user-centric approach in building meaningful and impactful software products.
